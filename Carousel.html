Calico Cats

A calico cat is a domestic cat of any breed with a tri-color coat. The calico cat is most commonly thought of as being 25% to 75% white with large orange and black patches

Ginger Cats

Though technically not a breed, ginger cats are known for their distinct coloration of varying shades of orange, red, and gold. With their good looks and friendly personalities, ginger cats are some of the most popular cats around.

Tabby Cats

A tabby cat is any domestic cat (Felis catus) with a coat pattern distinguished by an M-shaped marking on its forehead, stripes by its eyes and across its cheeks, along its back, around its legs and tail. Each is linked to specific genetics.

Art

Hobby art is the practice of creating visual art for personal enjoyment, relaxation, and self-expression. It includes activities like painting, drawing, sculpting, and photography, often done in one's free time without professional goals. Hobby art allows people to explore their creativity, relieve stress, and find joy in making something uniquely their own.

Gardening

Hobby gardening is the practice of growing and caring for plants for personal enjoyment, relaxation, and connection with nature. It can involve flowers, vegetables, herbs, or ornamental plants, and is often done in backyards, balconies, or community gardens.

Reading

Hobby reading is the joyful act of immersing oneself in books, articles, or stories for personal enrichment, entertainment, and mental escape. It’s a gateway to other worlds, ideas, and perspectives—whether you're flipping through a fantasy novel, exploring historical nonfiction, or diving into poetry.
